I grew up allergic to the baked goods everyone else could eat — dairy, nuts, and sesame were always off the table. That’s why I started Zachary’s Snackery: a brand that brings bold, nostalgic flavors to everyone in an allergy-friendly way. Every baked good is made without dairy, nuts, or sesame — so no one has to miss out on the good stuff.

Baked Goods

Gingersnaps

Classic spice with a crisp snap and warm molasses finish — perfect with a cup of tea or just on their own.

Chocolate Crinkle Cookies

Rich, fudgy chocolate cookies rolled in powdered sugar — cracked on the outside, soft on the inside.

Snickerdoodles

Soft, cinnamon-sweet, and kissed with gentle salt — a timeless favorite with an allergy-friendly twist. Also really good with coffee.
